About us

Clery Farm Foundation is a 501c3 non-profit that was formed to make an impact with regenerative agriculture through experiential, hands-on, self-directed learning. We're located in McMinnville, Oregon, at Clery Farm – an 80 acre property that is the site of the historic Cate's Farm.

Our team is comprised of dedicated educators, entrepreneurs, community members, and advisors in science, art & technology.
